# I built 10 apps in 10 months and make $800,000/yr (full guide)

A founder's journey from broke at 19 to $800K annual revenue through B2C app development.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Apps Generate True Passive Income** - Unlike SMMA work (constant sales calls, client meetings, time trading), apps are built once and sold forever

2. **First Mover Advantage Window** - B2C apps will create more millionaires than ecommerce; limited spots available; early action now = "underground bunker money" in 5 years

3. **Calculated Moves Over Luck** - First app "Snapout" made $20K in 30 days from 2 viral videos, but this wasn't luck—it was a set of calculated strategies

4. **Start with Validation** - Step 1: Don't waste time on unvalidated ideas; validate before building

5. **Shift from Time-Traded Revenue** - SMMA success revealed the trap of trading time for money; B2C apps solve this with subscription models
