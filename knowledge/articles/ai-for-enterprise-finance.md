Framework for applying AI agents to enterprise finance workflows. Rather than replacing entire ERP systems, the value is in automating the repetitive connective tissue between existing platforms — copying values between screens, reconciling data, chasing approvals, fixing mismatches. This gap represents 60% of finance team time and is where AI ROI actually exists.

## Key Takeaways

- Finance teams spend 60% of their time on manual connective tasks between systems (copy-paste, reconciliation, follow-ups)
- Don't replace your ERP — embed AI agents on top and between existing platforms your team already relies on
- The actual ROI is in automating the glue that holds disparate systems together, not in hypothetical futuristic finance brains
- Real outcome: reduced month-end close from 12 days to 5 days through intelligent automation
- This approach scales because it builds on existing infrastructure rather than competing with it
- Author is ex-Meta engineer now building Varick Agents for enterprise finance automation
