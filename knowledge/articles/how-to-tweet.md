## Summary

A practical guide to growing on Twitter based on 8 months of experience going from never writing tweets to having DMs full of business inquiries. The strategy is simple: post high-value content every single day. Covers the difference between brilliant insights and great tweets, the importance of genuine value, actionable advice, and organic engagement patterns.

## Key Takeaways

- **Genuine Value First** - A brilliant insight doesn't automatically make a great tweet. You need fresh, unstereotyped insights that haven't been repeated a thousand times on the platform.
- **Value Formula** - Post something new OR say something old in a way that makes people see it differently. Test: "Would I bookmark this if someone else wrote it?" If no, keep working.
- **Actionable > Entertainment** - "ChatGPT update is insane" gets views; "How to write copy that sounds human with GPT-5.2:" gets followers. Insight without blueprint is just entertainment.
- **Organic Engagement Strategy** - Don't beg for engagement. Structure tweets so bookmarks and replies happen naturally. Avoid "like if you agree" directly.
- **The Real Filter** - 8 months shows it's possible to build significant audience through pure value, but brilliance alone doesn't guarantee viral tweets—execution and timing matter.
