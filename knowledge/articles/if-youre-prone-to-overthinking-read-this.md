# If you're prone to overthinking, read this

Seven evidence-based concepts to limit overthinking and improve decision-making.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Morning Pages (Julia Cameron Method)** - Empty your mind through 45 minutes of unfiltered stream-of-consciousness journaling
2. **The 70-20-10 Rule** - Accept that 70% of work will be mediocre, 20% will fail, and 10% will be great; volume matters more than perfection
3. **Stop Looping with Powerful Questions** - When caught in mental loops, ask: "Am I present?", "Is this useful?", "Will I care about this in 1 year?"
4. **The 5 Second Rule** - Use the 5-4-3-2-1 countdown to override your brain's anxiety and activate your locus of control

The core insight: perfectionism and overthinking are linked to avoidance of failure, but failure is necessary for success. Creative breakthrough requires accepting imperfection.
