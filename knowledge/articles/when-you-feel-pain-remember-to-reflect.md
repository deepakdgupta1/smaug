## Summary

A reflection on handling financial portfolio pain and drawdowns. The author walks through their experience of a significant precious metals washout and frames it within the broader context of 6-sigma events. Rather than reactive panic, the article emphasizes the importance of stepping back to understand what actually happened, why it happened, how it impacted the portfolio, and where to go from here. The piece draws on lessons from Bridgewater about contextualizing challenges within the journey toward goals.

## Key Takeaways

- Drawdowns are inevitable on the path to financial goals; the importance lies in how you process them
- When market shocks occur, resist reactive commentary; most expert analysis is post-hoc rationalization
- Establish a standard framework for analyzing major events: What? Why? Impact? Next steps?
- Past pain informs future resilience; reflection transforms shock into learning
