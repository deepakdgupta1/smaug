## Summary

A deep exploration of what actually works when deploying AI agents in production at scale. Based on real experience deploying agents for enterprise customers, this article breaks down the gap between demo agents that work perfectly and production agents that fail. Key insight: most people don't understand what agents really are or how to build them that actually function.

## Key Takeaways

- **Context Is Everything** - Most agents fail because they lack proper context. Building agents is not about chaining tools together, but about intelligent context management.
- **Production vs Demo** - Agents that work in controlled demos hallucinate, forget mid-task, or call wrong tools in production environments.
- **The Real Formula** - After deploying agents for years at scale, there's a reproducible formula for what works, not just luck or being smarter than others.
- **Enterprise Deployment** - Company deployed production agents successfully, reached $3M ARR, proving it's about systematic approach, not magic.
