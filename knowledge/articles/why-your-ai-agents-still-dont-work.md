# Why your AI agents still don't work

Most AI agents fail because they lack feedback loops. RAG alone won't solve the fundamental integration and adaptation problems.

## Key Takeaways

1. **RAG is Not Enough** - Adding retrieval, memory, and "3 or 4 nifty engineering techniques" layered on top still won't solve core agent problems

2. **Feedback Loops Are Mandatory** - Every agent builder must embrace continuous feedback loops; agents improve tangibly over weeks to months with proper feedback

3. **Define Business Metrics First** - Step 0: Clarify your agent's end goal before building. What business metric will it lift? (retention, conversions, usage, etc.)

4. **Justify Existence with Real Results** - If your agent doesn't satisfy a genuine business outcome, it's a waste of time and resources

5. **Domain Knowledge Integration** - Agents struggle to integrate with domain-specific knowledge and adapt to feedback; this requires intentional architecture, not bolted-on solutions

6. **Long-Term Investment Required** - Building effective agents requires time investment and sustained improvement cycles

The insight: Agent success depends on clear objectives, feedback mechanisms, and continuous iteration—not just better engineering techniques layered on top of flawed foundations.
