Explores the paradox where AI tools increase individual developer productivity 5-10x but team velocity stays flat. The bottleneck isn't code production—it's human attention for review, coordination, and validation.

## Key Takeaways

- Individual AI gains don't scale to team output without addressing attention bottlenecks
- The Taskmaster case study: hundreds of AI-generated PRs, but only 3 maintainers could review
- Attention capacity is the real limiting factor in AI-accelerated teams, not code production
- Team coordination and review workflow become critical constraints when production velocity multiplies
- Traditional review processes can't keep pace with AI-generated work volume
