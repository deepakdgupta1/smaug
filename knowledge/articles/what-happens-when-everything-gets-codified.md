## Summary

An exploration of what happens when you systematically codify how you think and work. Joshua Schultz has been using Claude Code obsessively since summer, building hundreds of agents, nearly 1,000 skills, and thousands of scripts/templates. The insight: everything that can be codified will be codified, and when done comprehensively, it allows you to work at the speed of thought.

## Key Takeaways

- **The Codification Principle** - Everything that can be codified will be codified soon. Once you systematize your thinking, execution accelerates dramatically.
- **Hours Become Minutes** - Before codification: ideas take 30 minutes to 2 hours to execute (job description, memo, etc.). After: they execute as you're thinking about them.
- **Comprehensive Systems** - By codifying hiring philosophy, evaluation criteria, writing style, code architecture, business analysis, and leadership thinking, the system understands your perspective deeply.
- **Speed of Thought Execution** - Paired with all the references, templates, scripts, API calls, and skills, tasks move from conception to completion in parallel with thinking.
- **The Orchestration Economy** - Moving beyond traditional automation into a world where everything gets orchestrated through codified systems.
