## Summary

A deep exploration of how production AI development platforms (Cursor, Cognition, Anthropic, OpenAI) handle the complex infrastructure behind autonomous AI agents. Moves beyond polished demos to examine the real distributed systems challenges: sandboxing, real-time sync, isolation, checkpointing, recovery, semantic indexing, and the chaos engineering that enables "AI, fix this bug" to work at scale.

## Key Takeaways

- Production AI agent infrastructure requires distributed systems maturity beyond model capability
- Sandboxing, checkpointing, and recovery mechanisms are critical for reliability
- Real-time synchronization and isolation are non-trivial challenges
- Semantic indexing enables agents to understand context at scale
- The gap between demo and production is substantial
